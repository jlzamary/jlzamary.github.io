Algal Bloom Analysis in Coastal Alaska

Working with the IIC and WCS to analyze ice coverage and algal bloom dynamics in Chukchi and Beaufort Sea coastal lagoons using Python (GeoPandas, Pandas) and the Google Earth Engine API, producing time-series datasets for ecological monitoring.

Vector Analysis of Threatened Elephant Ranges

The threats to elephant populations in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Mozambique (referred to as KUTM Countries) were assessed to determine the legal protections available for these populations and to estimate both the proportion of elephants that are well protected and which specific populations are most threatened.

Jackalope Suitability Model (GIS 201 Final Project)

A suitability analysis was conducted for the mythical creature Jackalope in Wyoming, taking into account land cover, distance from roads, alcohol presence, and lightning pulse prevalence to provide data-driven policy decisions.
